1.Why You Should Consider Setting Up Your Own Social Network
  • They're highly engaging. Social networks tend to keep people coming back and sharing content, which means you don't need to work so hard on creating your own.
  • They are excellent channels for advertising. Your website will have a captive audience that often visits and is eager to provide personal information.
  • You can develop a vibrant community. The likelihood that two people will become closer increases with time spent together.

Once you find the perfect domain, you'll need to register it by filling out a few forms and paying for at least a year in advance.
